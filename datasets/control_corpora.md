# Control Corpora

## Purpose

Control corpora provide baselines for testing whether Voynichese statistics are unusual, language-like, generated, or dependent on preprocessing.

## Candidate Languages

Candidate comparison languages:

- Latin;
- Italian;
- German;
- English;
- Czech;
- Hebrew.

These are candidates only, not an approved final list.

## Candidate Text Types

- manuscript-like prose;
- recipes;
- medical or herbal texts;
- short generated text;
- random baseline text.

## Requirements

Each corpus must have documented source, date, license/status, preprocessing, and limitations.
